Featured Image AI generates DALL-E images when you publish a post without a featured image.

## How It Works

1. Click **Publish** on a post without a featured image
2. A panel appears in the pre-publish checklist
3. The panel detects any fallback images from SEO plugins (Yoast, RankMath)
4. Click **Generate with AI** to create a featured image
5. The AI analyzes your post title and content to generate a DALL-E prompt
6. If title overlay is enabled, the post title is composited onto the image
7. The generated image is saved to your media library

## Image Generation Defaults

Configure global defaults that apply to all generated images:

- **Image Style** — Photo-realistic, Illustration, Flat Design, Abstract, 3D Render, Watercolor, Minimal, Cinematic, Digital Art
- **Aspect Ratio** — Landscape (1792x1024), Portrait (1024x1792), Square (1024x1024)
- **Brand Colors** — colors the AI should incorporate
- **Custom Instructions** — additional generation rules

Authors can override these per-post in the editor.

## Title Overlay

Composite the post title onto the generated image for branded social sharing:

- **Position** — Bottom, Center, or Top
- **Background color** — semi-transparent overlay band
- **Text color** — title text color
- **Opacity** — 0% (transparent) to 100% (opaque)

## Cost

Each generation uses approximately **$0.04–0.08** in OpenAI API credits.
